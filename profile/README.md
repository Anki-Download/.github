## What is Borderless Gaming?

**Borderless Gaming** is an essential utility for modern PC gamers, designed to transform traditional fullscreen or windowed games into a seamless **borderless windowed mode**. This innovative approach eliminates the common frustrations associated with fullscreen gaming, such as significant delays, screen flickering, and application crashes when switching between your game and other desktop applications. By leveraging a borderless window, the software ensures that your game remains visibly stable and immediately accessible, even when you alt-tab to check a browser, respond to a message, or manage your streaming software. This is particularly crucial for multitaskers, content creators, and competitive gamers who require instant access to system resources without compromising their in-game performance or stability.

The core advantage of Borderless Gaming lies in its ability to make the gaming window behave as if it were in exclusive fullscreen mode, but without actually taking exclusive control of the display output. This results in a dramatically smoother and more fluid multitasking experience. For streamers and content creators, this means overlays, chat applications, and broadcast software can interact with the game window more reliably, preventing common issues like the game minimizing or capture sources failing. For everyday users, it simply means being able to pause a game, look up a guide, and return to the action without any hiccups, stutters, or black screens.

Supporting a vast library of PC titles, from indie gems to AAA blockbusters like **Minecraft** and **Metro Exodus**, Borderless Gaming has proven its versatility and reliability. Its straightforward, user-friendly interface allows even non-technical users to quickly add their games to a favorites list and apply the borderless setting with a single click. As a free and open-source project, it is continuously improved by a community of developers, ensuring ongoing compatibility with the latest Windows updates and game releases.
